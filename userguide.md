# Zoe — User Guide

Welcome to **Zoe**, a taste‑ranking app for slow curators. Use Zoe to discover great things, keep personal rankings, and share your point of view.

## Getting started

- **Open Zoe** after installing it from the App Store.
- **Browse first**: you can explore without doing anything special.
- **Create your profile (optional)**: add a photo and a short bio so people recognize you.

## The main tabs

Zoe is organized into five tabs:

- **Discover**: a curated feed of posts and recommendations.
- **Search**: find people, posts, and things to rank.
- **Rank**: your ranking lists and community rankings.
- **Shorts**: quick, immersive posts you can swipe through.
- **Profile**: your posts, shorts, rankings, and highlights.

## Discover

Discover is where you browse.

- Scroll to see new posts.
- Tap a post to open it and read the full details.
- If there are categories or topics, use them to narrow what you see.

## Search

Search helps you find what you’re looking for.

- Type a name or keyword to search.
- Tap a result to open it.
- Use Search when you want to add something specific to a ranking list.

## Rankings (Rank tab)

Rankings are the heart of Zoe. A ranking list is your ordered list of favorites in a category (for example: cafés, albums, shoes, perfumes).

### Create a new ranking list

1. Open **Rank**.
2. Choose **Create** or **New ranking** (wording may vary).
3. Pick a category and name your list.

### Add an item to a ranking list

1. Open **Rank** and select the list.
2. Tap **Add**.
3. Choose the item you want to add.
4. Compare it with a few items (you’ll be asked which you prefer).
5. Add a short note (optional) and confirm.

Zoe places the item into your list based on your comparisons, so you don’t have to guess a score.

### Reorder items

- Open the ranking list.
- Move an item up or down (the app may use drag‑and‑drop or a “Move” option).

### Edit or remove an item

- Open the ranking list.
- Tap the item for options such as **Edit note** or **Remove**.

## Shorts

Shorts are quick posts designed for fast browsing.

- **Swipe** to move to the next short.
- Use on‑screen actions to **Rank**, **Like**, **Comment**, or **Save** (if available).
- If you find something you love, open it for more details or add it to a ranking.

## Profile

Your Profile is your personal space.

- **Edit your profile**: update your photo, name, and bio.
- **View your content**: switch between posts, shorts, and rankings.
- **Highlights** (if available): pin or feature the things you care about most.

## Posting and sharing

Depending on what’s available in your version of Zoe:

- You may be able to create posts or shorts from your profile or a compose button.
- Keep your captions helpful: what it is, why it matters, and how it compares.

## Likes, comments, and saves

- **Like**: show appreciation for a post.
- **Comment**: ask questions, add context, or share your experience.
- **Save**: keep items for later so you can revisit them.

## Notifications (if available)

If you enable notifications, Zoe can alert you about things like:

- new comments on your posts
- activity from people you follow

You can change notification preferences in the app’s settings.

## Settings and preferences

Common settings include:

- **Account**: profile details and sign‑in options.
- **Notifications**: what you want to be alerted about.
- **Privacy**: control what others can see (if available).

## Tips for great rankings

- **Be consistent**: rank by your personal taste, not by hype.
- **Use notes**: a short reason helps you remember your choices later.
- **Revisit over time**: tastes change—updating a ranking is part of the fun.

## Troubleshooting

### The app feels slow or looks stuck

- Close Zoe and open it again.
- Make sure you have a stable internet connection.
- Try again in a few minutes if things still aren’t loading.

### I can’t find something I’m searching for

- Try simpler keywords (for example, just the main name).
- Check spelling.
- If there are multiple similar results, open each to confirm.

### I’m not seeing new content

- Pull down to refresh (if available).
- Make sure you’re online.

## Safety and comfort

Zoe works best when it stays thoughtful and respectful.

- Be kind and specific in comments.
- If something feels off, stop engaging and use any available reporting/blocking options.

## Help and support

If you need help:

- Check the app’s **Help** or **Support** section (if available).
- Use the **Contact** option in settings to reach support.

When you contact support, include:

- what you were trying to do
- what happened instead
- any on‑screen message you saw
